Chicken Recipe

Bihari style chicken curry cooked in mustard oil, onions and garlics are the major taste enhancers, onions are fried for at least 15 minutes and few garlics are added as whole, and then cook normally by adding garlic ginger paste and spices.

Ingredients

  • Chicken 750g
  • Muatard Oil
  • Onions
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Chicken Masala

Instructions

  1. Add mustard oil in pan.
  2. Roast onions for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and saute it.
  4. Add chicken and fry for 20-25 min on medium flame.
  5. Add chicken masala and mix it with water and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve it with Roti.
